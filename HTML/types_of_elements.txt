types of elemnts-
1) block elements
 --> these elemente always start on new line
--> occupues the entire width.
-->top and bottom margins exist

examples:
-div
-p
-h1-h6
-ul
-li
-blockquote
-dd
-dt
-dl
-tfoot
-address
-pre

2) inline elements
 --> these elemnts continue on same line.
-->these take as much as width is required
--> top and bottom do not exist

examples:
-span
-b
-abbr
-sup
-i
-q
